Mastering the Game

From Chess to StarCraft, AI has proven its ability to outthink human grandmasters in increasingly complex environments.

Milestones in AI Gaming

1997: Deep Blue

IBM's Deep Blue defeated world chess champion Garry Kasparov, marking the first time a computer beat a reigning world champion in a match.

2016: AlphaGo

DeepMind's AlphaGo defeated Lee Sedol in the game of Go, a feat previously thought to be a decade away due to the game's immense complexity.

2019: OpenAI Five

OpenAI Five defeated the world champion team OG in Dota 2, demonstrating cooperation and long-term planning in a real-time strategy game.

2019: AlphaStar

DeepMind's AlphaStar reached Grandmaster level in StarCraft II, mastering a game with imperfect information and high mechanical demands.
