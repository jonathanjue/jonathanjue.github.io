Predicting the Future

Large Language Models don't "know" things in the human sense. They are incredibly advanced statistical engines predicting the next word.

The "Next Token" Game

Imagine guessing the next word in a sentence. That's what LLMs do, billions of times a second.

1. Training Data

LLMs are fed massive amounts of text from the internet. They learn the statistical relationships between words (e.g., "King" is to "Queen" as "Man" is to "Woman").

2. Context Window

The "memory" of the model during a conversation. It looks at all the previous words (tokens) to understand the context before making a prediction.
